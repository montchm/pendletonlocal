# Business Plan: Local Digital Solutions
**Location:** Pendleton, Oregon  
**Structure:** Sole Proprietorship (Foundational Venture)  
**Document Version:** 1.0 (June 2026)

---

## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Mission Statement
To help local businesses in Pendleton strengthen community ties, modernise their digital footprint, and grow local revenue through simple, professional websites and consistent social media management.

### 1.2 Core Goal & Philosophy
This business serves as a low-risk, hands-on operational exercise in client management, project delivery, and business financial tracking. The goal is to build disciplined operational habits to lay the groundwork for future, larger-scale ventures. 

### 1.3 Financial Principles
* **Zero Debt**: 100% bootstrapped with zero outside liabilities.
* **Ultra-Low Overhead**: Fixed expenses are kept strictly near zero.
* **Sustainable Pace**: Growth is intentionally capped to protect service quality and allow deep learning.

---

## 2. Company Description & Risk Management

### 2.1 Legal & Operational Structure
The business operates as a **Sole Proprietorship** based out of Pendleton, Oregon. This structure offers maximum simplicity, lowest startup costs, and straightforward tax reporting via Schedule C. 

### 2.2 Risk Mitigation
To protect personal assets and maintain professional standards, the business maintains:
* **General Liability Insurance**: Protects against third-party bodily injury or property damage claims.
* **Professional Liability (E&O)**: Protects against claims of digital downtime, data errors, or service omissions.
* **Strict Contractual Boundaries**: Clear service level agreements (SLAs) dictating project scopes and communication hours to prevent scope creep.

---

## 3. Product & Service Offerings

To maintain high efficiency and clear client expectations, the service catalog is restricted to three flat-rate packages and a single hourly service.

| Package | Service Type | Price | Deliverables |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. The Digital Foundation** | Web Setup (One-time) | **$1,200** | 5-page mobile-optimized website, basic SEO, local photography, Google Business Profile setup. |
| **2. The Community Voice** | Social Media (Monthly) | **$450 / mo** | 2 platforms (FB/IG), 3 curated posts per week, local comment monitoring & engagement. |
| **3. The Pendleton Bundle** | Full Service (Monthly) | **$1,500 setup + $550 / mo** | Combines Packages 1 & 2. Includes ongoing website maintenance, hosting management, and monthly performance updates. |
| **Consulting & Training** | Hourly Service | **$65 / hr** | 1-on-1 strategy audit, digital roadmaps, or point-of-sale (POS) integration training. Minimum 2 hours. |

## 4. Market Analysis & The Pendleton Advantage

### 4.1 Target Market
Small, established, brick-and-mortar businesses in Pendleton (e.g., Main Street retailers, local diners, contractors, regional ranches, auto shops) featuring outdated websites or inactive social channels.

### 4.2 Competitive Edge
Rural business owners are traditionally skeptical of distant, automated tech agencies and aggressive cold callers. This business wins through:
* **Face-to-Face Trust**: In-person handshakes, local presence, and immediate community accountability.
* **Authentic Local Content**: Capturing real storefront photos and using local town context rather than generic, automated stock content.

---

## 5. Marketing, Sales & Capacity Strategy

### 5.1 The "Proof of Work" Engine
Because growth is intentionally restricted, standard outbound paid advertising is unnecessary. Marketing relies entirely on local reputation:
1. **Initial Proof Cases**: Build 2 or 3 high-quality website overhauls for well-known local organizations at a steep discount in exchange for public, local testimonials.
2. **Word of Mouth**: Leverage existing business networks, attend local Chamber of Commerce meetings, and use physical business cards with QR codes linking to the live proof of work.
3. **Relationship Sales**: Interact with prospective businesses as a local customer first, identifying operational gaps naturally before introducing services.

### 5.2 Capacity & Boundaries
To balance learning with execution quality, operational capacity is strictly capped at:
* **Maximum 3 Active Monthly Retainers**
* **Maximum 1 New Website Build** every two months.

## 6. Financial & Operational Plan

### 6.1 Low-Overhead Monthly Budget
The total fixed operational cost is capped at **$80.00/month**, rendering the business highly profitable from its very first client retainer.
